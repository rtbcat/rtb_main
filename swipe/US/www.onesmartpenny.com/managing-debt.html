Managing Debt

Your credit report is a detailed profile of your credit history, but it’s also a testimony to how well you’re managing debt. A history of on-time payments can let lenders know you manage debt well. Late or missed payments, bankruptcies, and repossessions can be a red flag for lenders.

People use many tactics to manage their debt. Some are effective and others less so. Before you apply for credit, it’s important to understand what’s on your credit report and how certain debt management strategies may affect your credit.
