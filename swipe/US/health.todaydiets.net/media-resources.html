Media Resources

Working Collaboratively with the Media

The American Red Cross has a presence in nearly every community in the United States and around the world. Many of the Red Cross chapters, Blood Services regions and stations have personnel immediately available to meet your media needs on a variety of different topics.
  • Blood and Biomedical Services
  • Domestic Disaster Response and Relief
  • Service to the Armed Forces
  • American Red Cross activities in other countries
  • Preparedness and Health and Safety Services
  • General Red Cross Questions or Issues

    • Media Contact Information

    If you are a member of the national or international media and have question about the national Red Cross, please call 202-303-5551 or email: media@redcross.org
    If you are a member of the local media, your local American Red Cross chapter is often the best source of information. Find your local Red Cross.
    If you have general questions about the Red Cross, please see Contact Us.
