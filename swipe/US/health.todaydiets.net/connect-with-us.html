Connect With Us

Red Cross Social Engagement

Connect and join a diverse group of people - across the U.S. and around the world!

Here at the American Red Cross, we see social media as a way for us to help people carry out the Red Cross mission every day. You can connect with us and other Red Cross supporters on the social networking sites you like to spend time on.

Red Cross Blog

The Red Cross Blog covers a diverse range of Red Cross related topics. We talk about things that move us, make us laugh, or connect us to other people. Also visit our Chapter Blogs and our Youth Blogs

Red Cross Blog

Facebook

Our Facebook community is full of supporters, volunteers, blood donors, and others from all walks of life who are looking for information on what we do.

Facebook

Twitter

Follow us on Twitter to get real time updates during disasters, as well as plenty of tips on how to be prepared and stay safe. Also Follow Your Local Red Cross on Twitter

Twitter

Instagram

Follow us on Instagram to access and share exciting images and videos about the Red Cross

Instagram

YouTube

Our YouTube channel is a library of all kinds of Red Cross videos, organized by topic. Stop by to learn about our latest disaster response or how to save a life with Hands Only CPR.

YouTube

LinkedIn

Find Red Cross colleagues and friends on LinkedIn, where you can connect in a more professional setting. You can also browse current job openings, and share them with your connections.

LinkedIn

Pinterest

Organize and share things you love on Pinterest. We share the most visually stunning images related to the Red Cross mission here.

Pinterest

Google+

Connect with us on Google+ and find Red Cross supporters who are interested in helping to make this world a little better place... one good deed at a time.

Google+

Flickr

Browse thousands of images on Flickr, uploaded by Red Crossers from across the country. We keep this updated frequently, especially as disasters strike.

Flickr

Sign Up for News

Stay informed on disaster alerts, preparedness tips, and ways to get involved.
