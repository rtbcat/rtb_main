Down the street, across the country, around the world—our work helps save lives.

With more than 23,000 employees and 330,000 volunteers, the American Red Cross comprises a diverse network of men and women committed to changing the world, one community at a time.

Where Your Talents Meet Our Opportunities

Explore our different lines of service to learn about career opportunities, then search for current job openings that match your interest and talents.

From phlebotomy to reference lab testing to donor recruitment, there are many ways to help us sustain and maintain the safety of our nation’s blood supply.

Through earthquakes, floods, tornadoes and home fires, we need specialists like you on the frontlines—coordinating response, implementing recovery and preparing for future events.

As a health and safety instructor or salesperson, your work ensures individual and community preparedness when emergencies strike.

Provide comfort and hope to our active-duty military, veterans and their families through overseas emergency communications, financial assistance and community referrals.

Whether garnering financial support, communicating our story or working behind the scenes, your contributions are the backbone of our organization.

Support our operations around the world—visit the International Federation of the Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies

Launch your career and gain real-world experience with one of the nation’s largest humanitarian non-profit organizations.



When you join the American Red Cross your hard work will help make people's lives better.

Accessibility

The American Red Cross is committed to working with and providing reasonable accommodation to individuals with disabilities. If you are in need of an accommodation while seeking employment, leave a voicemail at 202-303-8648. We will make a determination on your request for reasonable accommodation on a case-by-case basis. This number is for those with access or functional needs in need of accommodation only.
