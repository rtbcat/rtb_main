Welcome to RapidPass

Step 1 of 4: Introduction

RapidPass allows you to start the blood donation process from the comfort of your home or office and save time at the donation site. The process is simple, just read the following information and complete the questions online, then print your RapidPass and bring it with you or show it on your mobile device.

4 Steps to your RapidPass:

  1. Introduction
  2. Read the Education Materials
  3. Complete the Health History Questions
  4. Print or Email your Pass

Let's get started. A few things you should know:

  • The process will take approximately 10 to 15 minutes.
  • Please use the name listed on your donor card or legal name when logging in.
  • Our staff will discuss the questions with you and you may be asked to answer a question again or clarify an answer when you come for your donation.
  • RapidPass is not the same as an appointment. For the fastest donation experience possible, please complete your RapidPass and schedule a donation appointment.
  • If you plan to print your RapidPass, you will need Adobe Reader. Click Here for a free download.
 

Before proceeding, please read and verify the following statements by checking the boxes.

You must answer the questions the day of your donation.


You must answer the questions honestly and without influence from anyone else.


To ensure the safety of blood donation for both donors and recipients, all volunteer blood donors must be evaluated to determine their eligibility to give blood. The final determination will be made on the day of your donation at the blood drive or blood donation center.


You will need access to a printer or a mobile device. If you choose to print your pass, a laser printer is recommended. If you plan to pull up the pass on your mobile device, you will need access to an email account on that device.
