Wilderness and Remote First Aid

To provide individuals a foundation of first aid principles and skills to be able to respond to emergencies and give care in areas that do not have immediate emergency medical services (EMS) response. This includes wilderness and remote environments, including urban disasters, such as earthquakes and hurricanes. Participants must possess current adult CPR/AED certification (or the equivalent) and be at least 14 years of age on or before the last scheduled session of the course. Prerequisites will be verified by the instructor at the start of the course and are required for student participation in this course.

AED Training Programs for Schools

Prepare to keep your students and your faculty safe. When your school adopts American Red Cross AED, CPR and first-aid training exclusively for its health & safety training needs, our expert instructors will train one or more adults at the school as "instructors," who will deliver health and safety training programs to your students and faculty, under an "authorized provider" agreement between the school and the Red Cross.

Note that the FREE school offer waives student learner fees, but does not apply for any adult or instructor-training fees.

To get started, please call 1-800-RED CROSS and select the prompt for "Health and Safety Training."

The Red Cross makes AED certification fast, simple and easy. With Red Cross Digital Certificates you get anytime, anywhere access to your certificates; plus print, share, and download wherever and whenever you like. Digital certificates can be viewed, printed or shared online and can be accessed anytime through your Red Cross Account. Each certificate includes a unique ID and a QR code which meets employment requirements and allows employers to easily confirm your certificate is valid. Class participants and employers can visit redcross.org/confirm and enter the ID found on the digital certificate (or scan the QR code with a standard QR reader using a smart device) to access a copy of the valid certificate with student training information.
