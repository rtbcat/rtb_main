BLS Training for Healthcare and Public Safety

A better BLS product at a better price. Basic Life Support for Healthcare Providers (BLS) uses a scenario-based approach to develop the critical thinking and problem solving skills that drive better patient outcomes. Consistent with AHA Guidelines for CPR/ECC. Covers breathing and cardiac emergencies – including CPR, AED, and obstructed airway – for adult, child, and infant patients. Basic Life Support for Healthcare Providers - Classroom

In this 4.5 hour in-person training, our expert, certified instructors use real-life emergency response scenarios to help you learn how to perform CPR, use an AED, and relieve an obstructed airway for adult, child, and infant patients while also developing your critical thinking, problem solving, and team dynamics skills that drive better patient outcomes. Course fees include a free two-year digital certificate delivered instantly upon course completion. Click here to view the BLS Provider Handbook.

Basic Life Support for Healthcare Providers - Simulation Learning

This BLS course combines self-paced online learning with in-person skills practice and assessment with a certified instructor. Simulation learning is an interactive online experience where you will respond to real-life emergencies in a variety of healthcare and public safety settings. The online portion – including successful completion of the online cognitive assessment – must be completed prior to attending the in-person skills session. Course fees include a free two-year digital certificate delivered instantly upon course completion. Learners receive access to the online simulation for the duration of the certification period for additional practice or a self-paced refresher as needed. Red Cross Certification Made Simple. Fast. Easy. Digital.

The American Red Cross certification process is fast, simple and easy. With Red Cross Digital Certificates you get anytime, anywhere access to your certificates; plus print, share, and download wherever and whenever you like. Digital certificates can be viewed, printed or shared online and can be accessed anytime through your Red Cross Account. Each Red Cross certificate includes a unique ID and a QR code which meets employment requirements and allows employers to easily confirm your certificate is valid. Class participants and employers can visit redcross.org/confirm and enter the ID found on the digital certificate (or scan the QR code with a standard QR reader using a smart device) to access a copy of the valid certificate with student training information.
