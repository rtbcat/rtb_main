#TBT: In December 1948, Red Cross mobile first aid units traveled highways and by-ways across the United States. This vehicle designation meant that these drivers were trained and carried basic first aid equipment at all times. Drivers had permission from their employer to provide emergency care to accident victims while on their routes. Today, everyone can be armed with basic safety information with the help of our First Aid app: http://rdcrss.org/2mFRvND
