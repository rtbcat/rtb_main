Make a difference every day with a monthly gift.

Become a Red Cross Champion today.

Donate Now

Why making a monthly gift is a great choice:

  1. Most effective way to make a difference.
    The ongoing support of monthly donors, who we call Champions, helps to ensure that the Red Cross has resources to be there for families devastated by emergencies big and small, every single day.

  2. Easiest and most convenient way to give.
    Make one monthly gift today, and know your charitable giving is in place for the entire year to come - no further action required.

  1. Less stress in December
    By setting up your monthly gift today, you can relax in December knowing your tax-deductible giving plan is already in place for the end of the year.


  2. You can cancel at any time.
    If your situation changes for any reason, you can always change or cancel your gift with a simple phone call.



Become a Red Cross Champion

We respond to nearly 200 emergencies across the country every day.

That's an emergency every 8 minutes.
When you become a Champion, your monthly gift will be immediately put to work.

Provide hot meals and snacks for families in Red Cross emergency shelters.

Help hospital patients in need of lifesaving blood.

Connect families of US Armed Forces with their loved ones in times of crisis.

Train individuals in life-saving skills like CPR and water safety.

If you have questions about becoming a Red Cross Champion or would like to update your monthly donation, please contact us or call 1-800-RED CROSS
