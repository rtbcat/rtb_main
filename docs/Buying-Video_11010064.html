RTB Cat Help Wiki : Buying Video

Created by Jen Brannstrom, last modified on Jan 10, 2017

To get JS in-banner video ads approved on AppNexus you must:

  1. not rotate creatives
  2. the video must be able to play in any stream tester, eg:
    https://developer.jwplayer.com/tools/stream-tester/
    http://tagvalidator.tremorvideo.com/
    If it doesn'ty play there, it won't get approved
  3. make sure no blacklisted domains appear in the ad-call
  4. ask support to give you the latest whitelist for in-banner video, great placements with volume are: foxnews.com (Rubicon) and many more
